## Research Focus

Our capstone research centers on the design and evaluation of modular AI agent systems that support cybersecurity workflows in IoT and small-to-medium enterprise environments.

High-level areas of focus include:

- AI-assisted log and network traffic analysis  
- Human-in-the-loop security operations workflows  
- Retrieval-augmented learning systems  
- Explainable AI for defensible cybersecurity reasoning  
- Purple-team aligned investigative guidance  
- IoT-focused telemetry and analysis environments  

The system emphasizes analyst augmentation, workflow transparency, and standards alignment rather than fully autonomous threat detection.

Detailed architecture and technical implementation are maintained within the project-specific repository.
